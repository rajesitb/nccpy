NCC C Cert Online Exam Management Module

Developed by NER Dte NCC

This module is designed to hold NCC C Cert Exam online for cadets. The CO of a unit will plan the exam and schedule the exam for his cadets using this module. He can inform the cadets about their exam schedule by an email.

On the appointed day the CO will send an email link for the exam paper and a Google meet Link to the affected candidates. The link will be functional as per the schedule fixed by the CO. At the appointed time the candidate will activate his Google meet and the appointed invigilator can see live stream of his candidate. The candidate can now click on the exam link received in his email and commence answering the paper.

Following steps are involved for a CO:

  • Register on the site here

  • Login

  • Record unit data

  • Add Institutes and Cadets affected for NCC C Cert Exam

  • Divide cadets in unit wise batches for ease of conduct and invigilation. Review the batches and send email to cadets informing them of the schedule

  • Send Google meet id and exam link with time bracket for which the link will be valid

After conduct of exam is over, the results obtained by cadets can be viewed and printed

Question bank database and exam schema will be prepared by the Dte.

To practice the candidate, a sample paper will be available which can be sent to candidates for them to familiarize with the exam app.
